TECHNOLOGY ROADMAP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BiCS10 (Current Generation):
- 300+ layer 3D NAND
- Production ramp: Q1-Q2 2026
- Density: 2Tb per die
- Performance: Optimized for PCIe Gen 5
- Phison E26 compatibility: Validated

BiCS11 (Next Generation):
- 400+ layer 3D NAND
- Timeline: H2 2026 sampling, 2027 production
- Density: 3Tb per die
- Target: PCIe Gen 6 controllers
- Phison co-development: In progress

Joint Optimization:
- Controller firmware tuned for BiCS architecture
- Error correction optimized for BiCS characteristics
- Wear leveling algorithms BiCS-specific
- Performance benchmarking on BiCS NAND
