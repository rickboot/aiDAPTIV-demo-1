Dell Product Announcement - January 10, 2026

Dell today announced the XPS 15, featuring up to 128GB of DDR5 memory, targeting AI PC.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: XPS 15
- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB/64GB
- Processor: Intel Core Ultra
- Price: $2071
- Availability: Immediate

MARKET POSITIONING:
Dell positions the XPS 15 as a premium AI PC solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.

MEMORY CONSTRAINTS:
Soldered memory limits upgradeability

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS:
Supply chain pressures highlight need for alternative memory architectures
