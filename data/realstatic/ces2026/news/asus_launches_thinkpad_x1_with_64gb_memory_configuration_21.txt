ASUS Product Announcement - January 31, 2026

ASUS today announced the ThinkPad X1, featuring up to 64GB of DDR5 memory, targeting AI PC.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: ThinkPad X1
- Memory Options: 32GB/64GB
- Processor: Apple M4
- Price: $1721
- Availability: Immediate

MARKET POSITIONING:
ASUS positions the ThinkPad X1 as a premium AI PC solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.

MEMORY CONSTRAINTS:
Soldered memory limits upgradeability

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS:
Supply chain pressures highlight need for alternative memory architectures
