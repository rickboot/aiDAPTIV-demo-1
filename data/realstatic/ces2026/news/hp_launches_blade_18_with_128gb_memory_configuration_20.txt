HP Product Announcement - January 30, 2026

HP today announced the Blade 18, featuring up to 128GB of DDR5 memory, targeting Workstation.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: Blade 18
- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB/64GB
- Processor: AMD Ryzen AI
- Price: $4807
- Availability: Q2 2026

MARKET POSITIONING:
HP positions the Blade 18 as a premium Workstation solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.

MEMORY CONSTRAINTS:
Soldered memory limits upgradeability

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS:
OEM constraints create partnership opportunities for memory solutions
