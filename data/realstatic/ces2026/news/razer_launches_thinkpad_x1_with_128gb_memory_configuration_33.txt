Razer Product Announcement - February 12, 2026

Razer today announced the ThinkPad X1, featuring up to 128GB of DDR5 memory, targeting Enterprise.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: ThinkPad X1
- Memory Options: 32GB/64GB
- Processor: Intel Core Ultra
- Price: $1878
- Availability: Immediate

MARKET POSITIONING:
Razer positions the ThinkPad X1 as a premium Enterprise solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.
