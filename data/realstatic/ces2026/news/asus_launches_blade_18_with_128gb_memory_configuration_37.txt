ASUS Product Announcement - February 16, 2026

ASUS today announced the Blade 18, featuring up to 128GB of LPDDR5X memory, targeting Enterprise.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: Blade 18
- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB/64GB
- Processor: Intel Core Ultra
- Price: $4776
- Availability: Q1 2026

MARKET POSITIONING:
ASUS positions the Blade 18 as a premium Enterprise solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.

MEMORY CONSTRAINTS:
Soldered memory limits upgradeability

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS:
Premium pricing validates market willingness to pay for memory
