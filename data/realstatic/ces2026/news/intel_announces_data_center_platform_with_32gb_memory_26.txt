Intel Press Release - February 05, 2026

Intel today unveiled its Data Center Platform platform, featuring 32GB of HBM4 memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 32GB HBM4
