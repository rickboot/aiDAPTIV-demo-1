Dell Product Announcement - January 15, 2026

Dell today announced the XPS 15, featuring up to 128GB of LPDDR5X memory, targeting Enterprise.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: XPS 15
- Memory Options: 32GB/64GB
- Processor: Intel Core Ultra
- Price: $2453
- Availability: Q1 2026

MARKET POSITIONING:
Dell positions the XPS 15 as a premium Enterprise solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.
