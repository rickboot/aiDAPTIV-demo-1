Lenovo Product Announcement - February 23, 2026

Lenovo today announced the ROG Strix, featuring up to 64GB of DDR5 memory, targeting Workstation.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:

- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB
- Processor: AMD Ryzen AI
- Price: $3770
- Availability: Q1 2026

MARKET POSITIONING:
Lenovo positions the ROG Strix as a premium Workstation solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.
