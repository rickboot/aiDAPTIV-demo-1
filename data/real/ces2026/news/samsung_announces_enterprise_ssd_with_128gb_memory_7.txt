Samsung Press Release - January 17, 2026

Samsung today unveiled its Enterprise SSD platform, featuring 128GB of LPDDR5X memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 128GB LPDDR5X
- Bandwidth: 1600GB/s
- Target Market: AI Workloads
- Availability: Q2 2026
