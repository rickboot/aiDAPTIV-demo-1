Intel Press Release - February 20, 2026

Intel today unveiled its Data Center Platform platform, featuring 512GB of DDR5 memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 512GB DDR5
- Bandwidth: 800GB/s
- Target Market: AI Workloads
- Availability: H2 2026
