Intel Press Release - February 11, 2026

Intel today unveiled its AI Accelerator platform, featuring 32GB of LPDDR5X memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 32GB LPDDR5X
- Bandwidth: 1600GB/s
- Target Market: Edge Computing
- Availability: Q2 2026
