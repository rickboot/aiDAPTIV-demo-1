Lenovo Product Announcement - February 01, 2026

Lenovo today announced the XPS 15, featuring up to 32GB of LPDDR5X memory, targeting Enterprise.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: XPS 15
- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB/64GB
- Processor: Apple M4
- Price: $3337
- Availability: Q1 2026

MARKET POSITIONING:
Lenovo positions the XPS 15 as a premium Enterprise solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.

High memory configurations command premium pricing
