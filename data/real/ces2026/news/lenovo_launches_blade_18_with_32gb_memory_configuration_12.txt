Lenovo Product Announcement - January 22, 2026

Lenovo today announced the Blade 18, featuring up to 32GB of LPDDR5 memory, targeting Gaming.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:

- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB

- Price: $4292
- Availability: Immediate

MARKET POSITIONING:
Lenovo positions the Blade 18 as a premium Gaming solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.
