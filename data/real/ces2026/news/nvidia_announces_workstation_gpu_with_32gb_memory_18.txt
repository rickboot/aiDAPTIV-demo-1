NVIDIA Press Release - January 28, 2026

NVIDIA today unveiled its Workstation GPU platform, featuring 32GB of LPDDR5X memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 32GB LPDDR5X
- Bandwidth: 3200GB/s
- Target Market: Data Centers
- Availability: Q1 2026
