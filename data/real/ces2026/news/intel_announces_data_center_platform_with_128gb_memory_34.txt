Intel Press Release - February 13, 2026

Intel today unveiled its Data Center Platform platform, featuring 128GB of LPDDR5X memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 128GB LPDDR5X
- Bandwidth: 2400GB/s
- Target Market: Enterprise
- Availability: Q1 2026
