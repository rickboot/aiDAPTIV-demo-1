HP Product Announcement - February 14, 2026

HP today announced the Blade 18, featuring up to 32GB of DDR5 memory, targeting Workstation.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: Blade 18
- Memory Options: 16GB/32GB
- Processor: Apple M4
- Price: $2106
- Availability: Q2 2026

MARKET POSITIONING:
HP positions the Blade 18 as a premium Workstation solution, emphasizing memory capacity as a key differentiator.

MEMORY CONSTRAINTS:
Soldered memory limits upgradeability

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS:
Soldered configurations drive need for external memory solutions
