NVIDIA Press Release - January 11, 2026

NVIDIA today unveiled its AI Accelerator platform, featuring 64GB of LPDDR5X memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 64GB LPDDR5X

- Target Market: AI Workloads
