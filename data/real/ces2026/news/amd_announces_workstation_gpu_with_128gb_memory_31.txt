AMD Press Release - February 10, 2026

AMD today unveiled its Workstation GPU platform, featuring 128GB of LPDDR5X memory. The announcement comes as the industry grapples with increasing memory demands from AI workloads.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:
- Memory: 128GB LPDDR5X
- Bandwidth: 2400GB/s
- Target Market: Edge Computing
- Availability: Q1 2026
